ABOUT US

    Welcome to Books R Us, your number one source for all things books, e-books, articles and all your favorite reading materials. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of literatue, with a focus on productability, seamlessness, and resourcefulness Founded in 2022 by Mr. Jan Majed Shahata, Books R Us has come a long way from its beginnings in a Learning Resource Center. When Mr. Shahata first started out, his/her passion for providing readers with a plethora of books to choose from because of his love for reading all kinds of books may it be fictional or non-fictional, documented or researched drove him to work hard and quit his college degree, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over United States, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky wing of the book industry.

    We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
